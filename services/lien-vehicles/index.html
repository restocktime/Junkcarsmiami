Florida Lien Law Compliance

‚öñÔ∏è Operating Within Florida Legal Requirements

Florida Statutes Chapter 319 governs vehicle liens and transfers. As licensed auto dealers, we ensure full compliance:

All liens must be satisfied according to Florida Statute 319.27 before clear title can be issued.

All transactions comply with federal and state truth in lending requirements.
