Title and Legal Documentation Issues

üìÑ Common Title Problems with Inherited Vehicles

Deceased Owner Title

When the title is still in the deceased person's name:

  • Death certificate required: Official proof of death
  • Probate court order: Legal authority to transfer ownership
  • Affidavit of heirship: Proving inheritance rights
  • Florida DMV process: Specific state requirements
Lost or Missing Title

When the title cannot be located:

  • Duplicate title application: State process for replacement
  • Lien searches: Checking for outstanding loans
  • Heir authorization: Proving right to request duplicate
  • Alternative documentation: Registration, insurance records
Multiple Heirs

When several people inherit the vehicle:

  • Unanimous consent: All heirs must agree to sale
  • Executor authority: Court-appointed person can act alone
  • Buyout agreements: One heir purchasing others' interests
  • Legal documentation: Proper signatures and notarization
