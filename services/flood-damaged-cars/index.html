Frequently Asked Questions - Flood Damaged Cars

Will you buy my car if insurance declared it a total loss?

Yes! We regularly purchase vehicles that have been declared total losses by insurance companies due to flood damage. We handle all the paperwork.

How quickly can you remove a flood damaged car?

During hurricane season, we prioritize flood damage removals. We can typically provide same-day or next-day pickup, even during emergency situations.

Do you buy cars that were in salt water vs. fresh water?

We buy both! Salt water damage is typically more severe due to corrosion, but we purchase all flood damaged vehicles regardless of water type.

What if my flood damaged car won't start?

No problem! Most flood damaged cars won't start due to electrical or engine damage. We provide free towing and will remove your car regardless of its condition.
