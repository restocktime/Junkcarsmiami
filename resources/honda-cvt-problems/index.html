Honda CVT Failure Symptoms

üîç Early Warning Signs

Stage 1: Early Symptoms (50,000-80,000 miles)

  • Delayed acceleration: RPMs rise but slow speed increase
  • Whining noise: Especially during acceleration
  • Jerky movement: Hesitation when accelerating from stop
  • RPM fluctuation: Engine revs inconsistently
  • Burning smell: Overheated CVT fluid odor

Stage 2: Progressive Failure (80,000-120,000 miles)

  • Shuddering: Vibration during acceleration
  • Loss of power: Can't maintain highway speeds
  • Overheating: Temperature warnings
  • Slipping: RPMs increase without speed gain
  • Dashboard warnings: Check engine or transmission lights

Stage 3: Imminent Failure (120,000+ miles or severe cases)

  • Complete loss of power: Won't move in drive
  • Loud grinding: Internal component damage
  • Fluid leaks: CVT fluid puddles under car
  • Limp mode: Limited to one gear ratio
  • Total breakdown: Vehicle won't move
